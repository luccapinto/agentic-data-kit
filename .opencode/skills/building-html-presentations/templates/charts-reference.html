Chart cookbook

Copy a config into any starter. Choose by the question you're answering (see the comment at the top of this file), not by appearance. All charts use the brand tokens in :root.

Line

Trend over time, single metric.

Area

Trend with magnitude / cumulative feel.

Multi-line

Compare a few series over time.

Combo (bar + line)

Two units, e.g. volume vs rate.

Bar (vertical)

Compare categories.

Grouped bar

Compare categories across groups (plan vs actual).

Stacked bar

Composition within each category.

Horizontal bar

Rankings, long labels.

Scatter

Correlation between two metrics.

Bubble

Correlation + a third metric (size).

Pie / Doughnut

Part-of-whole (few slices).

Gauge

Single KPI vs a goal.

Radar

Multi-dimension profile.

Heatmap

Density across two dimensions.

Treemap

Hierarchy / share by size.

Funnel

Stage-by-stage drop-off.

Boxplot

Distribution & outliers.

Waterfall

Bridge of additive changes.
